Data Collection

FlixSkip does not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data whatsoever. We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics systems.

Local Storage

The extension stores your preferences locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.sync or browser.storage). This data never leaves your computer and is only used to remember your toggle settings.

  • Feature toggle states (on/off)
  • Statistics (skips count, time saved)

External Communications

FlixSkip makes zero external network requests. The extension contains no tracking pixels, no analytics scripts, no advertising SDKs, and no server communication of any kind.

Permissions Explained

  • storage — To save your preferences locally
  • activeTab — To detect skip buttons on the current tab
  • netflix.com access — The extension only activates on Netflix pages

Third-Party Services

FlixSkip does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or external resources. The extension is completely self-contained.

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data whatsoever, our extension is safe for users of all ages. We have no age restrictions.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
