FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Q

Is FlixSkip completely free?

Yes! FlixSkip is 100% free with no ads, no premium tier, and no hidden costs.

Q

Does FlixSkip collect my data?

No. FlixSkip does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All settings are saved locally in your browser.

Q

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave, Opera, and all Chromium-based browsers.

Q

How do I toggle features on/off?

Click the FlixSkip icon in your browser toolbar to open the popup and toggle each feature.

Q

Is this affiliated with Netflix?

No. FlixSkip is an independent project. It is not affiliated with Netflix in any way.
