Frequently Asked Questions

Is FlixSkip completely free?

Yes! FlixSkip is 100% free with no ads, no premium tier, and no hidden costs. Just install and enjoy.

Does FlixSkip collect my data?

No. FlixSkip does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All settings are saved locally in your browser. We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics.

Which browsers are supported?

FlixSkip works on Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, Brave, Opera, and all Chromium-based browsers. Safari support is coming soon.

How do I toggle features on/off?

Click the FlixSkip icon in your browser toolbar to open the popup. From there, you can toggle each feature (Skip Intro, Skip Recap, Still Watching, etc.) on or off.

Is this affiliated with Netflix?

No. FlixSkip is an independent, community-driven project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Netflix in any way.
